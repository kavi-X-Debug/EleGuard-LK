👋 What is EleGuard?

EleGuard is a mobile app that warns farmers when wild elephants are approaching their land — before it's too late. Using signals from ground sensors placed around the farm boundary, it detects elephant movement in real time and instantly alerts the farmer on their phone.

---

😟 The Problem

In rural Sri Lanka, elephants entering farmland at night is a serious and ongoing threat. Crops get destroyed, property gets damaged, and lives are put at risk — both human and elephant. Existing solutions like electric fences are expensive and often unreliable, leaving farmers with no early warning when an elephant is near.
---
🔬 How It Works

When an elephant walks, it creates seismic vibrations in the ground. Geophone sensors buried along the farm boundary detect these vibrations and measure their strength. EleGuard analyzes this data and classifies the threat:

Level	Meaning

🟡 LOW	Elephant detected far away
🟠 MEDIUM	Elephant getting closer
🔴 HIGH	Elephant at the boundary — act now!

📄 Based on real research: *Wijayaraja et al., IEEE Access, vol. 13, 2025*
